Dinosaurs ?

The name dinosaur comes from an english paleontologist named Richard Owen.

Dino comes from the greek "deinos" which means terrible

Saurus comes from the greek "sauros" which means lizard

Millions years ago / The Mezozoic Era

Dinosaurs are a group of reptiles that have lived on Earth for about 245 million years. They became extinct around 66 millions years ago.

The Mezozoic Era is also known as "The age of dinosaurs". During this era, there were differents period :

  • The Triassic
  • The Jurassic
  • The Cretaceous

Dino's Facts

There were more than 700 species.

They lived on all continents.

Many adult dinos were feathered and colorful.

What killed the dinosaurs?

It is believed that dinosaur extinction was part of a mass extinction brought about by two massive destructive events. The first of these was the collision with the Earth of a meteorite landing in what is now the Yucatan Peninsula, of Mexico. This was followed by an enormous volcanic eruption which split what is now India in half.
